Product Usage Instructions

Welcome to the Drawing Canvas! Follow these steps to start creating your masterpiece:

  1. Select your preferred brush size and color from the toolbar.
  2. Use your finger or stylus to draw on the canvas. Experiment with different techniques like shading, highlighting, and blending colors.
  3. When you're ready, save or share your artwork directly from the app.

Get creative and enjoy your drawing experience!
